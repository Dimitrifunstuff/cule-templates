# Cule Templates

Official templates for the [Cule](https://github.com/Dimitrifunstuff) browser —
reviewed and safe to use. Each one reshapes your browser: its look, its
behaviour, and sometimes extra tools.

The easiest way to browse and add these is **inside Cule** — open the Templates
panel and pick one.

## Available templates

- **Default** — a clean, no-frills browser. Re-add this anytime to get back to a
  safe starting point.
- **Research** — snip regions, clip pages, and organize your sources by session.

## Add one manually

Download a template's folder, then in Cule open the **Templates** panel →
**Import template** → pick that folder.
